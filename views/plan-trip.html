Plan Your Trip

Explore Pittsburgh with a ride on the historic Duquesne Incline. Whether you’re a first-time visitor or returning for another look at the city’s skyline, our website provides all the information you need.

Field Trip Program for Youth

The Duquesne Incline offers an exciting and educational field trip program for youth groups. Available for groups of ten or more (excluding weekends after 12:00 noon), the program includes:

  • A teacher’s lesson packet tailored to specific grade levels
  • A round-trip ride on the historic cable car
  • Insights into Pittsburgh’s industrial past and the history of inclines
  • An engaging visit to the machine room to observe original hoisting equipment
  • A panoramic view of downtown Pittsburgh from 400 feet above the river

City Tours

Enhance your visit with one of Pittsburgh's guided city tours. Choose from a variety of options:

  • Pittsburgh Movie Locations: Explore famous spots featured in local films.
  • Sports Tours: Visit iconic sports landmarks and stadiums.
  • Breweries & Wineries: Sample the city’s best local brews and wines while touring historical districts.
